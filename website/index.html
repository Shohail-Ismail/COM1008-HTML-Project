The Staff-Student Liaison Committee (SSLC) is the meeting point for students and their teachers where everyone sits down together to discuss school work, issues concerning subjects or just chat! Covering everything from sharing ideas, solving problems, and making sure everyone's voice is heard, this committee brings students and staff closer, thus creating a safe space where everyone is treated as person first, and a teacher/student next. In a nutshell, the SSLC serves as the main hub for students and teachers alike to converse and make the academic experience better for everyone.
