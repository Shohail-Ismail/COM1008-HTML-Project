About the Staff-Student Committee

The staff-student committee plays a crucial role in fostering collaboration between teachers and students, and serves as an extension of the learning community as a whole. We aim to not only provide a platform of correspondence between staff and students, but unite the two groups to allow for a synergistic, hospitable educational environment!

We can help you with:

  • Addressing concerns regarding academia

The primary mission of the Staff-Student Liaison Committee (SSLC) is to serve as a dedicated forum for students to address concerns related to academia, whether it be about coursework, teaching methods, or any other academic matter. By allowing students to raise their concerns with a committee member or with a teacher via a committee member in their field of study, any issues can be understood better and resolved/relayed with ease.

  • Points of communication between staff/students

The SSLC encourages and provides a medium for open and transparent communication between teachers and students, allowing for ideas, feedback, and suggestions to be exchanged and taken seriously. We do this by using representatives, through who you can speak to your teachers, thus creating a space where everyone's perspectives are valued and considered.

  • Help and advice with University life

While our student representatives' main role is to provide a mode of communication between teachers and students, they are also individuals with unique experiences and knowledge to whom you can directly voice any concerns or ask questions.

  • Or just having a chat!

Our purpose extends beyond academia and can provide a relaxed space where staff and students can chat with each other. Building a strong, interlinked community is one of the most important things to us and just remember that our main goal is to treat everyone as people before anything else!
